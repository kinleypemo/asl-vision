INSTRUCTIONS


1. To predict firstly you need to know the hand signs of all the alphabets except for J and Z ***

2. J and Z is left out because it needs dynamic data, where as only data is trained with image dataset.

3. To know about the hand signs you can refer the image aside

4. To predict you have to click on the predict button below

5. After clicking on the predict button a pop up will be shown where your web cam will be on.

6. After seeing the webcam on you have to show the alphabet sign that you want to detect.

7. The corresponding result of the hand sign you show will be display directly above your hand


*** Note: Only one hand will be detected. ***
